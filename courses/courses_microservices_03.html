Microservice 101 - Practiced Level

What you will learn

  • You will learn WHAT ARE the principles to microservice design
  • You will learn HOW-TO and WHY manage data storage
  • You will learn HOW-TO manage data the states of a microservice application
  • You will learn WHAT ARE the security principles
  • You will learn HOW-TO and WHY apply a certain level of security in a microservice architecture
  • You will understand WHAT are the main architecture pattern
  • You will learn WHY use them and WHEN use them

Target Audience

This course is especially designed for Developers, Freelancer, Team Leader, Technical Team Leader, Solution Architect and / or people which are directly involved in the implementation of a microservice architecture and services.

Duration

4 hours

Course Structure

Part 1

  • Design a Microservices
  • Design Guidelines
Part 2
  • Database
  • Stateless and Statefull services
  • Security
Part 3
  • API Gateway
  • Server-Side Discovery
  • CQRS
  • SAGA
  • Circuit Breaker
  • Logging
